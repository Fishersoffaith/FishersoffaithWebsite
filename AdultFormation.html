Exodus 90

Introdution of Exodus 90. Why Do Exodus 90:

  • Learn to find time for prayer (program’s aesthetic disciples) and ways of praying (daily prayer, Holy Hour, etc).
  • Develop / strengthen our relationship with God.
  • Develop fraternity with others.
  • Develop and live our faith through program discipline.
  • Become more patient, present, and lead family better.

We meet on Thursday evening (8 pm) at St. Teresa in the church basement.
