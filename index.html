What We Are About

We are Fishers of Faith, and we are unifying the St. Rose Family of Parishes into one community. We are achieving this by building our communities faith through asceticism, fraternity, and adult formation.

Goals

Facilitate a personal encounter and relationship with Jesus Christ.
Meet others wherever they are in their spiritual lives and help them take one step further.
Turn the hearts of parents to their spouse and children.
Form authentic leaders capable of transforming their families and greater society.
Form bonds of friendship and fellowship that strengthen the parish community.

Why

At our baptism, we were anointed into the ministry of Christ as priests, prophet, and king. We act as priests when we offer our lives as spiritual sacrifices to God. We become prophets when we bear witness to his goodness and live according to his ways. We are kings when we guide others to him and use our gifts to build up his Kingdom.
